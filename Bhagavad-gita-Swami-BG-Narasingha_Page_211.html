VERSE 10

वीजं मां सर्वभूतानां विद्धि पाथं सनातनम्‌ |

वुचि्धिमतामस्मि तेजस्तेजस्विनामहम्‌ ॥ ९०॥

bijarh math sarva-bbutanarh viddbi partha sanatanam

buddbir buddbimatam asmi tejas tejasvinam abam

O Partha, know that I am the original cause of all living

beings. lam the wisdom of the wise and I am the power of

the powerful.

1 am the might of the mighty which is devoid of passion

and attachment. I am the desire to procreate which does

not violate the principles of dharma.

VERSE 12,

ये चैव सात्तिका भावा राजसास्तामसाश्च ये ।

मत्त एवेति तान्विद्धि न त्वहं तेषु ते मयि ॥ १२॥

ye caiva sattvika bhava rajasas tamasas ca ye

matta eveti tan viddhi na tvabarh tesu te mayi

Also, know that all things generated by the modes of

goodness, passion and ignorance originate from Me alone.

However, I am not in them but they are in Me.
