In the modern age, the Vedic literature gives recom-

mendation for only one sacrifice and that is krsna-~saitktr-

tana in which one chants the maba-mantra, preceded by

the pattca-tattva mantra:

jaya Sri krsna caitanya, prabbu nityananda

jaya advaita gadadbara Srivasadi gaura-bhakta-vrnda

bare krsna hare krsna krsna krsna hare hare

bare rama bare rama rama rama bare bare

The chanting of the pafica~tattva mantra is to precede

the maba-mantra and frees the chanter from any previous

offences that may have been committed knowingly or

unknowingly. When the performance of krsna-sankirtana

is done alongside the distribution of Krsna maha-prasadam

it is considered perfect and complete. In the age of Kali-

yuga no other sacrifice is necessary.
