VERSE 25

भीष्मद्रोणपमुखतः सर्वेषां च महीक्षिताम्‌ ।

उवाच पाथं पश्येतान्समवेतान्कुरूनिति॥ २५॥

bhisma drona pramukbatab sarvesarh ca mabiksitam

uvaca partha pasyaitan samavetan kuraniti

In front of Bhisma, Drona and all the leaders of the world,

Sti Krsna said: O Partha (Arjuna, son of Prtha), behold the

Kaurava Dynasty assembled here!

VERSE 26

तत्रापच्यत्स्थितान्पार्थः पितृनथ पितामहान्‌ |

आचार्यान्मातुलान्घ्नातृ्पुत्ान्पोत्ान्सखीस्तथा |

ARTES सेनयोरुभयोरपि ॥२९॥

tatrapasyat sthitan parthab pitrn atha pitamaban

acaryan matulan bbratrn putran pautran sakbihs tatha

Svasuran subrdas caiva senayor ubbayorapi

There, between the two armies, Arjuna was able to observe

fatherly elders, grandfathers, teachers, maternal uncles,

brothers, sons, grandsons, fathers-in-law and friends.
