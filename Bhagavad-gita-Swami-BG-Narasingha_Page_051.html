VERSE 1

सञ्जय उवाच।

तं तथा कृपयाविष्टमशरुपूर्णाकुलेक्षणम्‌ |

विषीदन्तमिदं वाक्यमुवाच मधुसुदनः ॥ १॥

sanjaya uvaca —

tath tatha krpayavistam asru-pirnakuleksanam

visidantam idan vakyam uvaca madhusudanab

Saijaya said: Sti Krsna then spoke the following words to

Arjuna whose heart was overwhelmed with pity and whose

eyes were filled with tears.

VERSE 2

श्रीभगवानुवाच |

कुतस्त्वा कद्मलमिदं विषमे समुपस्थितम्‌ |

अना्य॑जुषटमस्वम्यमकीर्तिकरमजुन ॥२॥

Sri bhagavan wvaca —

kutas tva kaSmalam idath visame sammupasthitam

anarya-justam asvargyam akirti-karam arjuna

Bhagavan Sti Krsna said: Arjuna, how has such illusion

overcome you at this crucial moment? This is not appro-

priate for an honourable man, nor does it lead to higher

planets. It is the cause of infamy.
