ग BHAGAVAD-GITA oye

pasyadityan vasiin rudran asvinau marutas tatha

babuny-adrsta-purvani pasyascaryant bharata

0 descendant of Bharata, behold the Adityas, Vasus,

Rudras, the Agvini-kumatas and the Marutas. Behold the

multifarious amazing forms, never before seen.

VERSE 7

इहैकस्थं जगत्कृत्स्ं A सचराचरम्‌ ।

मम देहे गुडाकेश यचचान्यद्रष्टूमिच्छसि ॥७॥

ibaikastharh jagat krtsnarh pasyadya sa-caracaram

mama debe eudakesa yac canyad drastum icchasi

9) conqueror of sleep, behold in this one place the entire

cosmos, including all moving and non-moving beings, all

within this form of Mine along with whatever else you

desire to see.

aq मां शक्यसे द्रष्टुमनेनैव स्वचक्षुषा ।

Yet you are unable to perceive this with your present eyes,

therefore I will give you divine vision. Now behold My

mystic splendour!
