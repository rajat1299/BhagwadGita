VERSE 66

सर्वधर्मान्परित्यज्य मामेकं शरणं वरज ।

अहं त्वं सर्वपापेभ्यो मोक्षयिष्यामि मा शुचः ॥६६॥

sarva~dharman parityajya mam chart Saranatih vraja

aban tvarh sarva~papebbyo moksayisyami ma Sucab

Abandon all types of dharma — come and surrender unto

Me alone! Do not fear, for I will surely deliver you from

all reactions.

Anuvttti

In verse 63 Sti Krsna says that He has disclosed to

Arjuna the most confidential knowledge and tells Arjuna

to deliberate upon it and then do as he wishes. But because

Atjuna is very deat to Krsna and because Krsna is Atju-

na’s gura and well-wisher, Krsna again gives him one last

instruction and assurance.

Krsna’s final instruction is that Arjuna should always

fix his mind upon Him, he should devote himself fully to

Him, worship Him and offer his respects unto Him. This

is the essence of Krsna consciousness. Krsna says that by

doing this Arjuna will surely come to Him. The ultimate

insttuction to always remember Krsna is also stated in

Padma Purana as follows:

smartavyab Satatarh visnur vismartavyo na jatucit

sarve vidhi-nisedbab syur etayor eva kinkarab

Sti Krsna (Visna) should always be remembered and

never be forgotten at anytime. All rules and regula-

tions mentioned in the Sastra should be subservient

to these two principles. (Padma Purana 6.71.100)
