VERSE 10

अपर्यासं तदस्माकं बलं भीष्माभिरक्षितम्‌ ।

पर्या त्विदमेतेषां बरं भीमाभिरक्षितम्‌ ॥ ९०॥

aparyaptarh tad asmakarh balarn bbismabbiraksitam

paryaptarh tv idam etesarn balarh bhimabbiraksitam

Our army, protected by the might of Bhisma is unlimited.

However, the strength of the opposition, protected by

Bhima is insufficient.

VERSE 11

अयनेषु च सर्वेषु यथाभागमवस्थिताः।

भीष्ममेवाभिरकषन्तु भवन्तः सर्वं एव हि ॥ ११॥

ayanesu ca sarvesu yatha-bhagam avastbitab

bhismam evabbiraksantu bhavantab sarva eva bi

You must support and protect Bhisma at all costs, at the

strategic points of our battle formation.
