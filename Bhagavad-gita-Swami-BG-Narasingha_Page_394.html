VERSE 19

सो मामेवमसम्मूढो जानाति पुरुषोत्तमम्‌ |

स सर्वेविद्धनति मां सर्वभावेन भारत ॥ १९॥

yo mam evam asammidbo janati purusottamam

sa sarva-vid bbajati marh sarva-bbavena bharata

O Bharata, whoever is free from bewilderment knows Me

as the Supreme Person. Such a person knows everything

and worships Me with all his heart.
