ग BHAGAVAD-GITA oye

mac-cittab sarva-durgani ma t-prasadat tarisyasi

atha cet tvam abankaran na Srosyasi vinanksyasi

By My mercy, all your troubles will be overcome if you

think of Me. However, if out of false ego you ignore Me,

you will perish.

VERSE 59

यदङ्कारमाभित्य न योत्स्य इति मन्यसे ।

मिथ्यैष व्यवसायस्ते प्रकृतिस्त्वां नियोक्ष्यति ॥५९॥

yad abankaram asritya na yotsya iti manyase

mithyaisa vyavasayas te prakrtis tvatit niyoksyati

If due to false ego you think, “I will not fight,” your deci-

sion will be useless because your very nature will urge you

to do so.
