arjuna uvaca —

kith tad-brabma kim adbyatmarh kirh karma purusottama

adbibbutarh ca kith proktam adbidaivarh kim ucyate

adbiyajftah katharh ko’tra debe’smin madbusudana

prayana-kale ca katharh fiteyo’st niyatatmabbib

Arjuna inquired: O Purusottama (Supreme Person), what

is Brahman? What is the self? What is karma? What is

action? What is this material manifestation? Who are the

demigods? Who is the object of sacrifice and how does

He dwell within the body? O Madhusidana, how can

You be known by those who are self-controlled at the

time of death?
