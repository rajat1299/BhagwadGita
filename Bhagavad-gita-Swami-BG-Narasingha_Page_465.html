Sti Krsna now gives Arjuna an infusion of encoutage-

ment by stating that by following His instructions Arjuna

will realise the Absolute Truth and will enter His Supreme

Abode. By consciously offering the results of all his actions

unto Krsna, by devoting himself to Krsna and always think-

ing about Krsna, Arjuna will achieve all perfection.

The alternative, Krsna says, is that by neglecting His

instructions, Arjana will surely perish. This is the open

secret of Bhagavad-gita — one who adhetes to its message,

that is free from all material defects and is delivered by

the Supreme Person, Sti Krsna, becomes perfect in knowl-

edge and self-realisation. Neglecting Krsna’s instructions is

however tantamount to spititual suicide and Krsna warns

Arjuna that if he takes that path he will surely perish into

the world of bewilderment, illusion and death.
