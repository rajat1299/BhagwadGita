VERSE 27

यावत्सज्ञायते किञ्चित्सत्त्वं स्थावरजङ्गमम्‌ ।

aa कषेतरज्ञसंयोगाततदिदधि भरतर्षभ ॥२७॥

yavat sathjayate hiftcit sattvarn sthavara ~jangamam

ksetra-ksetrajfia-sarhyogat tad viddbi bharatarsabba

You should understand that whatever exists, whether

moving or non-moving, is simply manifest from the com-

bination of the field and the knower of the field, O best of

the Bharata Dynasty.

VERSE 28

समं सर्वषु मृतेषु तिष्ठन्तं परमेश्वरम्‌ ।

विनरुयत्स्वविनरुयन्तं यः परुयति स परुयति ॥२८॥

samarh sarvesu bbitesu tisthantarh paramesvaram

vinaSyatsv-avinasyantaih yab pasyati sa paSyati

One actually sees when he perceives the Supreme Control-

ler as being situated in all living beings, and realises that

neither the Super Consciousness nor the individual unit of

consciousness is perishable.

VERSE 29

समं पञ्यन्डहि सर्वत्र समवस्थितमीश्वरम्‌ ।

न हिनस्त्यात्मनात्मानं ततो याति परां गतिम्‌ ॥२९॥

(1.1, 1111110. 11111.

By seeing the Supreme Person situated in all places equally,

one does not become degraded. Thus he attains the

Supreme Abode.
