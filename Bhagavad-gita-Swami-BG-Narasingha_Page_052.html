O Partha, give up this unmanliness. It does not befit you.

O chastiser of enemies, get up and do not yield to this

petty weakness of heart.

VERSE 4

अजुन उवाच |

कथं भीष्ममहं संख्ये द्रोणं च मधुसुदन |

इषुभिः प्रतियोत्स्यामि पूजार्हावरिसूदन ॥४॥

arjuna uvaca —

katharh bbismam abarh sankbye dronath ca madbusidana

isubbib pratiyotsyami pujarhav-arisudana

Arjuna replied: How can I counterattack such persons as

Bhisma and Drona in battle, firing arrows at those who are

worthy of my respect, 0 Madhusitidana?

VERSE 5

Tee हि महानुभावान्‌ श्रेयो भोक्तु भक्ष्यमपीह रोके ।

हत्वाथकामीस्तु गुरूनिदैव भुञ्जीय भोगानुधिरपरदिग्धान्‌ ॥५॥

gurin abatva bi mabanubbavan

Sreyo bhokturn bhaiksyam aptha loke

batvartha kamarnstu guran ihaiva

bhufijtya bhogan rudbira-pradigdhan

It is better to live in this world by begging than kill-

ing our respectable superiors. Otherwise, the wealth and

property that we enjoy here in this world will be tainted

with their blood.
