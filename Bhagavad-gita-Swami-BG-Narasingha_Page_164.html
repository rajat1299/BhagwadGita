VERSE 10

बह्मण्याधाय कर्माणि सङ्ग ATA करोति यः ।

किप्यते न स पापेन पद्यपत्रमिवाम्भसा ॥ ze ll

brabmany-adhaya karmani अती tyaktva karoti yab

lipyate na sa papena padma-patram ivambbasa

One who gives up all attachments and acts by offering all

his actions unto the Supreme is never contaminated by any

impiety, just as a lotus leaf is never touched. by water.

VERSE 11

कायेन मनसा TaN केवलैरिन्दरियैरपि ।

योगिनः कमं कुर्वन्ति सङ्ग त्यक्त्वात्मशुद्धये ॥ ११॥

kayena manasa buddhya kevalair indriyair api

yoginah karma kurvanti sangarh tyaktvatma Suddbaye

By giving up all attachments, the karma-yogi performs

actions through the medium of the body, mind, intelligence

and senses simply for the purpose of self-purification.

VERSE 12

युक्तः SS त्यक्त्वा शान्तिमाप्ोति नेठिकीम्‌ |

अयुक्तः कामकारेण फे सक्तो निवध्यते ॥ १२॥

-yuktab karma-phalarh tyaktva Santim apnoti naisthikim

ayuktab kama-karena phale sakto nibadbyate

The karma-yogi, giving up the results of his actions, attains

everlasting peace. However, the selfish worker being

attached, desires the results of his actions and therefore

becomes ensnared.
