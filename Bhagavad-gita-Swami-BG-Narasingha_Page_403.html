VERSE 7

प्रवृत्तिं च निवृत्तिं च जना न विदुरासुराः ।

Those who are asuras by nature cannot discriminate as to

what actions should be done and what actions should not

be done. No purity, proper behaviour or truthfulness can

be found in them.
