VERSE 12

तस्य Geren कुरुवृद्धः पितामहः |

सिंहनादं AA: शद्ध दध्मो प्रतापवान्‌ ॥ १२॥

tasya saitjanayan barsarh kuru-vrddhab pitamabab

sitnha-nadarh vinadyoccaib Sankbarn dadbmau pratapavan

Then Bhisma, the fearless grandsire of the Kuru Dynasty,

roaring like a lion, blew his conch loudly to increase the

euphoria of Duryodhana.

tatab Sankbas ca bheryas ca panavanaka-gomukbab

At that point, conches, trumpets, bugles, drums and horns

suddenly sounded all at once and the combined. sound rose

up like thunder.
