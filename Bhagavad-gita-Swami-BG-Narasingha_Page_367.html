VERSE I

श्रीभगवानुवाच |

परं भूयः प्रवक्ष्यामि ज्ञानानां ज्ञानमुत्तमम्‌ ।

यज्ज्ञात्वा मुनयः सरवे परां सिद्धिमितो गताः ॥९॥

Srt-bhagavan wvaca —

pararh bhiyab pravaksyami jiananarh filanam uttamam

yaj fiatva munayab sarve pararh siddbim ito gatab

Bhagavan Sti Krsna said: I will now describe to you that

knowledge which is the greatest of all types of knowledge.

By knowing this, all the sages were able to attain perfection

and teach the highest destination.

VERSE 2

इदं ज्ञानमुपाश्रित्य मम साधर्म्यमागताः ।

Bish नोपजायन्ते प्रख्ये न व्यथन्ति च ॥२॥

idarh fanam upasritya mama sadharmyam dgatab

sarge’pi nopajayante pralaye na vyathanti ca

By taking shelter of this knowledge, one attains My nature.

He is neither born during cteation nor does he suffer

during universal annihilation.

Anuvttti

In previous chapters the modes of matetial nature,

goodness, passion and ignorance (sattva-guna, raja-guna

and tama-guna) have been mentioned and in this chap-

ter they will be described in greater detail. It will also be

described how one can cross beyond the material modes

and become free from the cycle of birth and death.
