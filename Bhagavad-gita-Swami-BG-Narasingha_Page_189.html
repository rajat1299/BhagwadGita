VERSE 25

शनैः THOTAGEM धृतिगृहीतया |

आत्मसंस्थं मनः कृत्वा न किचिदपि चिन्तयेत्‌ ॥२५॥

Sanaib Sanair uparamed 1

atma-saristharh manab krtva na kificid api cintayet

Gradually, one should still the mind by means of the intel-

ligence, focusing it on the self and nothing else.

VERSE 26

यतो यतो निश्चकति मनश्चञ्चलमस्थिरम्‌ ।

ततस्ततो नियम्यैतदात्मन्येव वहं नयेत्‌ ॥२६॥

yato yato niscalati mana§ caficalam asthiram

tatas tato niyamyattad atmany-eva vaSath nayet

The nature of the mind is flickering and unsteady. How-

ever, one should always endeavour to control the mind

from its wanderings and bring it back under the control of

the higher self (atma).
