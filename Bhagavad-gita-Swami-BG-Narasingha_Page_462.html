विमुच्य निर्ममः शान्तो ब्रह्मभूयाय कल्पते ॥५६॥

buddbya visuddbaya yukto dbrtyatmanarh niyamya ca

Sabdadin visayarhs tyaktva raga-dvesau vyudasya ca

vivikta-sevi laghv-asSi yata-vak-kaya-manasab

dhyana-yoga-paro nityarh vairagyarh samupasritab

abankararh balan darparh kamarh krodbarh parigrabam

virnucya nirmamab Santo brabma-bhiyaya kalpate

With pure intelligence, controlling the mind with deter-

mination, abandoning attachment to sense-objects, being

devoid of both attachment and hatred, residing in a

secluded place, eating little, controlling speech, body

and mind, being constantly engaged in meditating on the

Supreme Person, being renounced, free from egotism, the

misuse of power, conceit, lust, anget, covetousness and.

being unselfish and peaceful — such a person is qualified

for realisation of the Absolute Truth.

equally, he attains transcendental devotion unto Me.
