Anuvttti

The fate of the world is ultimately sealed, but the

opportunity for the conditioned living beings to attain

enlightenment and transcend the world of birth and

death is open to all. Sti Krsna say, kalo’sni loka-ksaya-

kyt — “Time I am, the mighty destroyer of worlds.” This

is the fate of the world. Time is the great enemy and it

eventually devours all things. This insurmountable time

is the energy of Krsna.

masartu-darvi-parighattanena

suryagnina ratri-divendbanena

asmin maba-mobamaye katabe

bbutani kalab pacatiti varta

The twelve months and six seasons are like cooking

spoons. The sun is the fire for cooking. Day and

night are the fuel consumed by the sun. Ignorance is

the cooking pot and the living beings are within that

pot being cooked by time. That is the way of this

world! (Mahabharata, Vana-parva 313.118)
