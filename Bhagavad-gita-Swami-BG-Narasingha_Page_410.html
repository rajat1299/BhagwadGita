yah Sastra-vidhim utsrjya vartate kama-karatab

na sa siddbim avapnoti na sukbarh na pararh gatim

He that neglects the rules of the Vedas in order to satisfy

his material desires, never attains perfection, happiness, or

the Supreme Abode.

VERSE 24

The Vedic injunctions are your authority pertaining to

what is to be done and what is not to be done. Thus, real-

ising your duty in this world, you should act accordingly.
