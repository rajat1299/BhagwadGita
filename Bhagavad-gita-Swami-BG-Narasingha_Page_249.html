VERSE 1

श्रीभगवानुवाच |

इदं तु ते गुह्यतमं प्वक्षयाम्यनसूयवे ।

ज्ञाने विज्ञानसहितं यज्ज्ञात्वा मोक्ष्यसेऽशुभात्‌ ॥ १॥

Sri bhagavan wvaca —

idarn tu te gubya tama pravaksyamy-anasityave

franamn vijfiana-sabitarh yaj fhatva moksyase’ subbat

Bhagavan Sti Krsna said: Because you are not envious I

shall now explain to you the greatest of all secrets. I shall

explain this knowledge and it’s realisation, knowing which

you shall become free from all inauspiciousness.

VERSE 2

राजविद्या राजगुह्यं पवित्रमिदमुत्तमम्‌ |

प्रत्यक्षावगमं धर्म्यं सुसुखं कर्तुमव्ययम्‌ ॥२॥

111 1/1 pavitram idam uttamam

pratyaksavagamarh dbarmyarh susukbarh kartum avyayam

This is the greatest of all knowledge (raja-vidya@) and the

greatest of all secrets (raja~gubya). It is most purifying and

most excellent. This path of dharma is directly perceived;

it is simple to practice and imperishable.
