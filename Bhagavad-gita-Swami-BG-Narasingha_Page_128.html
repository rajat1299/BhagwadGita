ग BHAGAVAD-GITA oye

afo’pi sannavyayatma bhutanam-isvaro’pi san

prakrtirn svam-adhisthaya sathbbavamy-atmamayaya

Though I am unborn and My form is imperishable, and

though I am the Controller of all things, I still remain in

control of My material energy and manifest through My

own potency.

VERSE 7

यदा यदा हि धर्मस्य ग्लानिर्भवति भारत |

अभ्युत्थानमधर्मस्य तदात्मानं सृजाम्यहम्‌ ॥७॥

yada yada bi dharmasya glanir bhavati bharata

abbyutthanam adbarmasya tadatmanam srjamyabam

O descendant of Bharata, whenever there is a decline in

dharma anda rise of adbarma, I personally appear.

VERSE 8

परित्राणाय साधूनां विनाशाय च दुष्कृताम्‌ |

धरम॑संस्थापनाथांय संभवामि युगे युगे ॥८॥

paritranaya sadbinarm vinaSaya ca duskrtam

dharma-sarnsthapanarthaya sarnbhavami yuge yuge

To protect the pious living beings and to put an end to

malevolence, I appear in every age to establish dharma.

VERSE 9

जन्म कर्म च मे दिव्यमेवं यो वेत्ति तत्त्वतः ।

त्यक्त्वा देहं पुनज॑न्म नैति मामेति सोऽजुन ॥९॥

janma karma ca me divyam evath yo vetti tattvatab

tyaktva debarh punar-janma naiti mameti so’rjuna
