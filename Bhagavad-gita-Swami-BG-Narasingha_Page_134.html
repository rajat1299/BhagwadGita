VERSE 10

वीतरागभयकोधा मन्मया ATT: ।

बहवो ज्ञानतपसा परता मद्धावमागताः ॥ १०॥

vita-raga-bhaya-krodba man-maya mam-upasritab

bahavo jfana-tapasa puta mad-bhavam agatab

Freed from mundane attachment, fear and anger, and

absorbed in thinking of Me, many people have taken refuge

in Me and become purified by the knowledge of austerity

and attained love for Me.

VERSE 1

ये यथा मां प्रपयन्ते तांस्तथैव भजाम्यहम्‌ ।

मम वर्मानुवर्तन्ते मनुष्याः पार्थसर्वंशः ॥ ९१॥

ye yatha mar prapadyante व bhajamy-abam

mama vartmanuvartante manusyab partha sarvasab

In whatever way people surrender unto Me, I reward them

accordingly. O Partha, all men follow My path.

VERSE 12

aged: कर्मणां सिधि यजन्त इह देवताः ।

क्षप हि मानुषे लोके सिचिर्भवति कर्मजा ॥ १२॥

kanksantab karmanarh siddbirh yajanta iba devatab

ksiprarn bi manuse loke siddbir bhavati karmaja

In this world, those that desire material success worship

the demigods, since in human society success from such

activities is quick to manifest.
