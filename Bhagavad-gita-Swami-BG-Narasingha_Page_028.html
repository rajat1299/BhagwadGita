Behold, O great teacher, the military formation of the

army of the sons of Pandu arranged. by your gifted student,

Dhrstadyumna, the son of Drupada.

VERSE 4

अत्र दूरा महेष्वासाभीमाजुंनसमायुधि ।

युयुधानो विराटश्च द्रुपदश्च महारथः ॥४॥

atra Sura mabesvasa bhimarjuna-sama yudbi

yuyudbano viratas ca drupadasca mabarathab

Within those ranks are great archers who are equal to

Bhima and Arjuna in war such as Satyaki, Virata and the

powerful charioteer Drupada.
