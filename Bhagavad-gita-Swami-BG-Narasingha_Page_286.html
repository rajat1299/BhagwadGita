VERSE 38

दण्डो दमयतामस्मि नीतिरस्मि जिगीषताम्‌ |

मौनं चैवास्मि गुह्यानां ज्ञानं ज्ञानवतामहम्‌ ॥३८॥

dando damayatam asmi nitir asmi figisatam

maunatm caivasmi gubyanamh jana jAanavatam abam

Iam the punishment enforced by those who chastise. I am

the conduct of those that desire victory. I am the silence of

secrets and I am the knowledge of the wise.

VERSE 39

yac capi sarva-bhutanar bijarh tad abam arjuna

na tad asti vind yat syan maya bbutarh caracaram

I am the seed of all living beings, O Arjuna. There is noth-

ing moving or non-moving, that can exist without Me.
