VERSE 5

अन्तकाले च मामेव स्मरन्मुक्त्वा कलेवरम्‌ |

यः प्रयाति स मद्धावं याति नास्त्यत्र संशयः ॥५॥

anta-kale ca mam eva smaran muktva kalevaram

yah prayati sa mad-bhavarh yati nasty-atra sarhSayab

At the time of death, one who leaves the body specifi-

cally remembering Me attains My nature — of this there

is no doubt.

VERSE 6

यं यं वापि स्मरन्भावं त्यजत्यन्ते कलेवरम्‌ |

तं तमेवेति कोन्तेय सदा तद्भावभावितः ॥६॥

yath yath vapi smaran bbavam tyajaty-ante kalevaram

tar tam evaiti kaunteya sada tad-bhava-bhavitah

O son of Kunti, whatever state of being one remembers at

the time of death, one certainly attains to that nature.

VERSE 7

तस्मात्सर्वेषु काटेषु मामनुस्मर युध्यच |

म्यर्षितमनोवुच्िमांमेवैष्यस्वसंशयः ॥७॥

tasmat sarvesu kalesu mam anusmara yudbya ca

mayy-arpita-mano buddbir mam evaisyasy-asathsSayab

Therefore, always remember Me and fight! Surrender your

mind and intelligence to Me and you will surely attain Me.
