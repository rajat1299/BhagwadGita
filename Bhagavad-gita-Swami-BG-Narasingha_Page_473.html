so’pi muktab subbanl-lokan prapnuyat punya-karmanam

Those who hear this sacred conversation with transcenden-

tal faith and without envy will achieve perfection and reach

My auspicious Abode.

Anuvttti

Herein, Sti Krsna says that those who are envious

cannot be taught the science of the Absolute Trath. How-

ever, those with the fortitude of Krsna consciousness, who

study this sacred conversation between Krsna and Arjuna

and who teach this knowledge to the non-envious are dear

to Krsna and will achieve perfection and attain Krsna’s

abode. This is confirmed in the following verses of the

Gita-mabatmya and the Vaisnaviya-tantra-sara:

gita-Sastram idarh punyarh yab pathet prayatah puman

visnob padam avapnoti bhaya Sokadi varfitab

One who tecites the Bhagavad-gita, which bestows

all virtue, with resolute devotion will attain to the

Supreme Abode of Visnu/Krsna, which is always

free from the mundane qualities based on fear and

lamentation. (Gita-mabatmya 1)
