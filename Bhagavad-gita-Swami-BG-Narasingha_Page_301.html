I see that You are without beginning, middle or end. You

have unlimited power and innumerable arms. Your eyes

are the sun and the moon. This entire universe is scorched

by the rays emanating from Your mouth like blazing fire.

Anuvttti

The universal form of Sti Krsna is one that invokes

great awe, reverence and laudations from the beholder,

but factually speaking, a devotee of Krsna is not charmed

by such a display. Awe and reverence borders on feat and

as we shall see, Arjana does indeed become fearful as he

continues to behold Krsna’s universal form. Fear does not

encourage a loving relationship with the Supreme. There-

fore, the universal form of Krsna is not very important for

students of bhakti-yoga, only in as much as it proves to

demonsttate that unless one can show the universal form,

one should not be accepted as God.

Factually, there are different avataras of Krsna that

appear in the universe at scheduled times and these have

been mentioned by Jayadeva Gosvami as follows:
