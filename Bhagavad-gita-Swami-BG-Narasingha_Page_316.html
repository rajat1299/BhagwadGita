VERSE 43

पितासि लोकस्य चराचरस्य त्वमस्व पूज्यश्च गुरुर्गरीयान्‌ ।

न त्वत्समोऽस्त्यभ्यधिकः कुतोऽन्यो ोकत्रयेऽप्यप्रतिमप्रभाव | GF It

pitasi lokasya caracarasya

tvamn asya pijyas ca gurur gartyan

na tvat-samo’sty-abbyadbikab kuto’nyo

loka-traye’py-apratima-prabbava

You are the father of all moving and non-moving beings in

the universe. You are the most venerable and most glorious

teacher. You have no equal within all the three worlds.

How can there be anyone greater than You, O Master of

incomparable power?

VERSE 44

तस्मात्मणम्य प्रणिधाय कायं प्रसादये ATS ।

पितेव पुत्रस्य सखेव सख्युः प्रियः प्रियायाहसि देव सोढुम्‌ Nee

tasmat pranamya pranidbaya नी

prasadaye tvam abam iSam idyam

piteva putrasya sakbeva sakbyub

priyab priyayarbasi deva sodbum

Therefore, O Master, I prostrate myself in front of You

and beg You to be merciful unto me. O Krsna, kindly for-

give me, as a father, friend or lover would forgive a son, a

friend or a beloved.
