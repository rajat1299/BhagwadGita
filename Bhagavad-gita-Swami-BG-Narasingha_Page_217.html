Whether one is without desire or full of desire or

Krsna further explains that pious persons (the dis-

tressed, the inquisitive, those seeking wealth and those

desiring self-realisation) take shelter of Him, but of these

Kysna says that those in search of knowledge and self-real-

isation ate superior.

After taking shelter of Sti Krsna for wealth, out of cari-

osity ot out of distress, it is often seen that such persons

again resume their normal course of material activities.

Those who come to Krsna for knowledge and self-realisa-

tion truly become liberated from material desires and enter

into a plane of eternal bliss. Ultimately they attain Krsna’s

Supreme Abode, never to return to the world of birth and

death as will be explained in Chapter 15. Therefore, the

seekers of self-realisation who take shelter of Krsna and

absorb all their thoughts and deeds in Krsna are considered

the best among virtuous persons.
