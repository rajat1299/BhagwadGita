aR उवाच |

सेनयोरुभयोममध्ये रथं स्थापय मेऽच्युत ॥२९१॥

यावदेतान्निरीक्षेऽहं योद्ुकामानवस्थितान्‌ ।

कैर्मया सह योद्धव्यमस्मिन्‌ रणसमुद्यमे ॥२२॥

arjuna uvaca —

senayor ubbayor madbye ratharh sthapaya me’cyuta

yavad etan nirikse’barh yoddhu-kaman avasthitan

kairmaya saba yoddbavyam asmin rana-samudyame

Arjuna said: O Acyuta (Infallible One), place my chariot

between both armies so that I may look upon the soldiers

that I must do battle with.

Let me see all those warriors that are dear to Duryodhana, the

wicked son of Dhrtarastra, assembled here for this battle.
