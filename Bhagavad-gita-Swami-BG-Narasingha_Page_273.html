VERSE 8

अहं सर्वस्य प्रभवो मन्तः सर्व परवर्तते ।

इति मत्वा भजन्ते मां वुधा भावसमन्विताः ॥८॥

abath sarvasya prabbavo mattab sarvath pravartate

iti matva bbajante marh budba bhava-samanvitab

Tam the source of everything. All things emanate from Me.

Understanding this, the wise who are endowed with love,

worship Me with all their heart.
