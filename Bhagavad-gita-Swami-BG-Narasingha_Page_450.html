9) Dhanaijaya, please hear as I now describe to you in

to the three modes of nature.

VERSE 30

परवृत्तिं च निवृत्ति च Sree भयाभये ।

aed मोक्षं च या वेत्ति वृद्धिः सा पार्थं सा्तिकी ॥६०॥

pravrttin ca nivrttirn ca karyakarye bhayabbaye

bandbarh moksarh ca ya vetti buddbib sa partha sattvikt

0 Partha, the mentality in the mode of goodness is that

which can distinguish what should be done and what should

not be done, duty and non-duty, what is to be feared and

what is not to be feared and the nature of material bondage

and liberation.

VERSE 31

यया धर्ममधर्मं च कार्यं चाकार्यमेव च ।

अयथावत्मरजानाति GPa: सा पाथं राजसी Re

yaya dharmam adharmarh ca karyarh cakaryam eva ca

ayathavat prajanati buddbib sa partha rajast

O Partha, the mentality in the mode of passion cannot

distinguish between dharma and adbarma, cannot discern

what is right and what is wrong, and cannot decide what is

duty and what is not duty.
