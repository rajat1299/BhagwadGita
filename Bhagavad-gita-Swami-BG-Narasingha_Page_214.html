In the ontology of mantras it is said that ort ema-

nates from the flute of Sti Krsna; it then manifests as

gayatri-mantra, then as the Vedas, Vedanta and Srimad

Bhagavatam. The last verse of Bhagavatam recommends the

chanting of the maha-mantra.

nama-sankirtanam yasya Sarva~papa-pranasanam

pranamo dubkha-Samanas tarh namami barith param

The chanting of the maba-mantra can relieve us from

all undesirable habits, all filthy characteristics and

all miseries. Chant the maba-mantra! Nothing else

is necessaty. Take this! Chant the maha-mantra and

begin your real life in this dark age of Kali-yuga with

the most broad and wide theistic conception. Let

us all bow down to Sti Krsna. (Srimad Bhagavatam

12.13.23)

One may chant om and certainly everyone should chant

the maba-mantra. Those who do this under the guidance

of the spiritual master will certainly taste the fruits of

self-realisation.
