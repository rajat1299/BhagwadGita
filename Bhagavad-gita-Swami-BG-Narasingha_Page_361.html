VERSE 33

यथा सर्वगतं AAS AAA |

सर्वत्रावस्थितो देहे तथात्मा नोपकिप्यते ॥३३॥

ya tha Sarva-gatamn sauksmyad वु nopalipya te

sarvatravasthito debe tathatma nopalipyate

Just as the subtle element of all-pervading space does not

mix with anything, similarly, the individual unit of con-

sciousness does not mix with the material body, although

it is situated within.

VERSE 34

यथा प्रकारायत्येकः Hea लोकमिमं रविः |

केतं क्षेत्री तथा कृत्सं प्रकाशयति भारत ॥६४॥

yatha prakasayaty-ckab krtsnarh lokam imarh ravib

ksetrarh ksetri tatha krtsnarh prakasayati bharata

O Bharata, just as one sun Uluminates the entire universe,

the occupier of the field illuminates the whole field.
