अ CHAPTER 1 Aye

VERSE 1

धृतराषट उवाच |

धर्मक्षेत्र कुरक्षेत्र समवेतायुयुत्सवः।

मामकाः पाण्डवाश्चैव किमकुर्वत सञ्जय ॥१॥

dhrtarastra uvaca —

dharma-ksetre kuru-ksetre samaveta yuyutsavah

mamakab pandava§ caiva kim akurvata safijaya

Addressing Safjaya, Emperor Dhrtarastra said: After

assembling with great enthusiasm for battle at the holy

place of Kuruksetra (dharma-ksetra), what did my sons and

the sons of Pandu do?

VERSE 2

सञ्जय उवाच |

दक्ष तु पाण्डवानीकं व्यू दुर्योधनस्तदा |

आचा्यमुपसम्य राजा वचचनमनवीत्‌ ॥२॥

sanjaya uvaca —

drstva tu pandavantkarh vyadbarh duryodbanas tada

acaryam upasatigamya raja vacanam abravit

Safijaya replied: © Emperor, at that time your son Dury-

odhana, after observing the military arrangements of the

Pandavas, approached his mentor Drona and spoke as

follows.
