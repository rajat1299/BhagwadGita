Sri bhagavan wvaca —

aksararh brabma paramarh svabbavo'dhyatmam ucyate

bhuta-bhavodbhava karo visargah karma-sarhjititab

Bhagavan Sti Krsna replied: It is said that Brahman is

the imperishable Supreme, and the atma is the original

spiritual nature of the living being. Karma is that which

produces birth, duration of life and death.
