VERSE 6

यथाकाशस्थितो नित्यं वायुः सर्वत्रगो महान्‌ ।

तथा सर्वाणि भूतानि मत्स्थानीत्युपधारय ॥६॥

12.11.11

tatha sarvani bbutani mat-sthanity-upadbaraya

Try to understand that just as the mighty wind rests

within the vast expanse of the sky, similarly all beings

rest within Me.

VERSE 7

सर्वभूतानि कोन्तेय प्रकृतिं यान्ति मामिकाम्‌ |

कल्पक्षये पुनस्तानि कल्पादौ विसृजाम्यहम्‌ ॥७॥

11.111 kaunteya prakrtir yanti mamikam

kalpa-ksaye punas tant kalpadau visrjamy-abam

O son of Kunti, at the end of a day of Brahma, all living

beings enter into Me. I manifest them again at the beginning

of a new creation.

VERSE 8

प्रकृतिं स्वामवष्टभ्य विसृजामि पुनः पुनः ।

भूतयाममिम Pearse HpaI, ॥८॥

1 01 avastabbya visrjami punab punab

bhuta-gramam imath krtsnam avasam prakrter vaSat

Because I control material nature, I continuously manifest the

living beings that are helplessly manipulated by their natures.
