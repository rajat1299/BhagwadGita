VERSE 32

अधर्मं धर्ममिति या मन्यते तमसावृता ।

स्ार्थान्विपरीतांश्च वद्धिः सा पार्थं तामसी RR

adbarmarh dharmam iti ya manyate tamasavrta

sarvarthan viparitarns ca buddbih sa partha tamasi

O Partha, the mentality in the mode of ignorance regards

that which is adbarma to be dharma and that which is

dharma to be adharma. It regards everything to be the

opposite of reality.

VERSE 33

धृत्या यया धारयते मनः प्राणेन्द्रियक्रियाः ।

योगेनाव्यभिचारिण्या धृतिः सा पार्थं सात्त्विकी ॥३३॥

dbrtya yaya dharayate manab pranendriya-kriyab

-yogenavyabbicharinya dhrtib sa partha sattvikt

O Partha, that determination by which one strictly con-

trols the mind, life-airs and the senses through the process

of yoga is in the mode of goodness.
