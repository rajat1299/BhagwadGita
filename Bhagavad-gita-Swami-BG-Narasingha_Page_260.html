Those who are conversant in the Vedas reach the higher

planets and enjoy celestial pleasures. Krsna says that when

their pious credit is exhausted, they again enter the mortal

plane of Earth. Therefore, the understanding is that all

endeavours for material pleasure are temporary, even those

experienced in higher planets.

But for the bhakti-yogi there is no need to perform com-

plicated and costly Vedic rituals because all the purposes

of such ceremonies are fulfilled by acts of devotion and

surrender to Krsna. The intimacy of the bbakti-yogis with

Krsna is such that He proclaims that He carries what they

ack and preserves what they have. This is the extension of

Kysna’s shelter (asraya), for those who sutrender to Him.

This theme is repeated throughout the Bhagavad-gita.
