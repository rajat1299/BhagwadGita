VERSE 9

सञ्जय उवाच |

एवमुक्त्वा हृषीकेशं गुडाकेशः परंतप |

न योत्स्य इति गोविन्दमुक्त्वा तुष्णीं बभूवह ॥९॥

sanjaya uvaca —

evan uktva brstkesarn oudakesab parantapa

na yotsya iti govindam uktva tusnirh babbuvaba

Saijjaya said: Having thus addressed Sti Krsna, the vigi-

lant conqueror of enemies Arjuna declared, “O Krsna, O

Govinda, I will not fight!” and became silent.

Descendant of Bharata, there, between the two armies, St

Krsna (Hrsikesa) smiled and spoke the following words to

the grief-stricken Arjuna.
