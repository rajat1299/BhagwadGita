VERSE 28

येषां त्वन्तगतं पापं जनानां पुण्यकर्मणाम्‌ \

ते दंमोहनिर्गक्ता भजन्ते मां डताः ॥२८॥

011 jananarh punya-karmanam

te dvandva-moba-nirmukta bhajante marh drdha-vratab

Yet those that perform pious activities become purified of

all reactions — they become free from the illusion of duality

and worship Me with dedication.

VERSE 29

जरामरणमोक्षाय मामाधित्य यतन्ति ये ।

ते ह्य तद्विदुः PATH कमं चाखिलम्‌ ॥२९॥

jara-marana-moksaya mam asritya yatanti ye

te brabma tad vidub krtsnam adbyatmarh karma cakbilam

Those that strive to become free from old age and death by

taking shelter of Me know the Absolute Truth, the indi-

vidual self and the laws of action and reaction.

VERSE 30

साधिभूताधिदैवं मां साधियज्ञं च ये विदुः ।

प्रयाणकालेऽपि च मां ते विदुर्यक्तचेतसः ॥३०॥

sadbibhutadhidaivarh mah sadbiyajftarh ca ye vidub

prayana-kale’pi ca marh te vidur yukta-cetasab

Those who know Me to be the Controller of matter, the

Controller of the demigods and the recipient of all sacri-

fices — with their minds concentrated upon Me, they will

know Me at the time of death.
