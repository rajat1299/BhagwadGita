VERSE 49

दूरेण ह्यवरं करम बुद्धियोगाद्नञ्जय ।

Fal शरणमन्विच्छ कृपणाः फरुदेतवः ॥ ४९॥

direna hy-avararh karma buddbi-yogad dhanaftjaya

buddhau Saranam anviccha krpanab phala-betavab

9) Dhanaajaya, fruitive activities are by far inferior to the

yoga of wisdom. Therefore, take shelter in the wisdom

of equanimity. Those that are motivated by the fruitive

results of their actions are misers.

VERSE 50

वुचचियुक्तो जहातीह उभे सुकृतदुष्कृते |

तस्माद्योगाय युज्यस्व योगः कर्मसु कौशलम्‌ ॥५०॥

buddbi-yukto jabatiha ubbe sukrta-duskrte

tasmad yogaya yujyasva -yogab karmasu kausalam

A wise man refrains from performing both good and bad

actions in this world. Thus, engage in yoga, as yoga is the

best of all activities.

VERSE 51

कर्मजं वुचियुक्ता दि फलं त्यक्त्वा मनीषिणः ।

जन्मवन्धविनिमुक्ताःपदं गच्छन्त्यनामयम्‌ ॥५९॥

karma-jarn 0440 yukta bi phalarh tyaktva manisinab

janma-bandha-vinirmuktab pada gacchanty-anamayam

The wise give up the results of their actions and thus lib-

erate themselves from the bondage of material birth and

death. Thus they attain the plane beyond all suffering.
