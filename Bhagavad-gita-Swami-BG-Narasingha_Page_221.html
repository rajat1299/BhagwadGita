Undoubtedly, Visnu (Krsna) is the only deliverer of

liberation for all. (Padma Purana 6.253.176)

When surrender to Krsna is the only shelter from the

cycle of birth and death, then it stands to reason that one

should take shelter of Krsna and forego the worship of

vatious demigods.

to be the impersonal that has now accepted a material form.
