VERSE 18

यदा विनियतं चित्तमात्मन्येवावतिषठते |

निस्पृहः सर्वकामेभ्यो युक्त इत्युच्यते तदा ॥ ९८॥

yada viniyatarh cittam atmany-evavatisthate

nisprhab sarva-kamebbyo yukta ity-uchyate tada

When the steady mind is fixed exclusively upon the self,

then one becomes free from all material desires — such a

person is said to be situated in yoga.
