VERSE 30

मयि सर्वाणि कमणि संन्यस्याध्यात्मचेतसा ।

निरा्ी्निमंमो भूत्वा युध्यस्व विगतज्वरः ॥३०॥

mayi sarvant karmani sannyasyadhyatma-cetasa

nirasir nirmamo bbutva yudbyasva vigata-jvarab

Completely surrendering all your activities unto Me, with

your consciousness fully situated in the self, without any

selfish motivation, without any sense of ownership and

without grief -- fight!

VERSE 31

ये मे मतमिदं नित्यमनुतिष्टन्तिमानवाः।

श्रद्धावन्तोऽनसूयन्तो मुच्यन्ते तेऽपि कर्मभिः ॥३९॥

11/10 anutisthanti manavab

Sraddbavanto’nasiyanto mucyante te’pi karmabbib

Those that faithfully follow these instructions of Mine

without envy will become free from the bondage of actions.

VERSE 32

ये त्वेतदभ्यसूयन्तो नानुतिष्ठन्ति मे मतम्‌ ।

सर्वज्ञानविमूढस्तान्विद्धि नष्टानचेतसः ॥३२॥

11111 1 acetasab

However, you should know that those persons who, out

of envy, do not follow My instructions are deprived of all

knowledge. They have lost sight of the goal of life and are

bereft of intelligence.
