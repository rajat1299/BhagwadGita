VERSE 10

योगी युञ्जीत सततमात्मानं रहसि स्थितः ।

एकाकी यतचित्तात्मा निराश्चीरपरिग्रहः ॥ १०॥

yogi 11111111 0.1.12.

chakt yata-cittatma 11

A yogi should live in a solitary place with his mind and

body fully controlled. He should be without desire, with-

out a sense of possessiveness and must constantly fix his

mind on the atma, the self within.
