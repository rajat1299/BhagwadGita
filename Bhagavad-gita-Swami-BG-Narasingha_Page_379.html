One who works free of attachment is in the mode

of goodness. One who works with personal desire

is in the mode of passion. One who works without

discrimination of right and wrong is in the mode

of ignorance. But one who takes shelter of Krsna

is understood to be transcendental to the modes of

material nature. (Srimad Bhagavatam 11.25.26)

The liberated person is always situated in bhakti-yoga

without deviation because he knows Sti Krsna as the
