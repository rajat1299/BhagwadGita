VERSE 20

आसुरीं योनिमापन्ना मूढा जन्मनि जन्मनि |

मामप्राप्यैव कोन्तेय ततो यान्त्यधमां गतिम्‌ ॥२०॥

॥.. yonim apanna miadba fjanmani janmani

mam aprapyaiva kaunteya tato -yanty-adbamart gatim

O Kaunteya, continually taking birth amongst the impious,

such foolish petsons never attain Me. Rather, they fall to

the most abominable destinations.

VERSE 21

त्रिविधं नरकस्येदं ah नाशनमात्मनः ।

कामः कोधस्तथा कोभस्तस्मादेतच्चयं त्यजेत्‌ ॥२९॥

(1/1 11/21.

kamab krodbas tatha lobbas tasmad etat trayarh tyajet

There are three paths leading to the lower planets and

self-destruction — lust, anger and. greed. Thus, these three

must be abandoned because they are the great destroyers

of self-realisation.

VERSE 22

पतेर्विमुक्तः कोन्तेय THEA: ।

आचरत्यात्मनः भरेयस्ततो याति परां गतिम्‌ ॥ २२॥

etair virnuktah kaunteya tamo-dvarais tribbir narab

acaraty-atmanab Sreyas tato yati pararh gatim

© Kaunteya, one who is free from these three paths of

darkness acts in his best interest. He gradually teaches the

Supreme Abode.
