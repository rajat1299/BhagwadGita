VERSE 1

अजुन उवाच।

ज्यायसी चेत्कर्मणस्ते मता FRISIAN |

तत्किं कर्मणि घोरे मां नियोजयसि केशव ॥१॥

arjuna uvaca —

fyayasi cet karmanas te mata buddbir janardana

tat kitn karmani ghore math niyojayast keSava

Arjuna said: O Janardana, O KeSava, if it is Your opinion

that wisdom is better than action, then why do You wish

to engage me in such violent activities?

VERSE 2

व्यामिश्रेणेव वाक्येन वुद्धि मोहयसीव मे |

तदेकं वद्‌ निश्चित्य येन भ्रेयोऽहमापुयाम्‌ ॥२॥

vyamisreneva vakyena buddbith mohayasiva me

tad ckarh vada niscitya yena Sreyo’ham apnuyam

Your words seem to be contradictory and my mind is now

confused. Therefore, kindly tell me which particular path

is most beneficial for me.

VERSE 3

श्रीभगवानुवाच।

लोकेऽस्मिन्दिविघा निष्ठा पुरा परोक्ता मयानघ |

ज्ञानयोगेन सांख्यानो कर्मयोगेन योगिनाम्‌. ॥३॥

Sri bhagavan wvaca —

loke’smin dvi-vidha nistha pura prokta mayanagha

jhana~yogena sankbyanarn karma~yogena Yyoginam
