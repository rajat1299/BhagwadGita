ग BHAGAVAD-GITA oye

ramya kacid upasana vraja-vadbu

vargena ya kalpita

Srimad-bhagavatarh pramanam amalarh

prema pumartho maban

Sri-caitanya-mabaprabbor matam idarh

tatradaro nab parab

The Supreme Person, Sti Krsna and His transcen-

dental realm of Vendavana ate the most worship-

pable objects. The supreme method of worshipping

Him is that adopted by the beautiful gopis, the young

wives of Vraja. Srimad Bhagavatam is the most pure

and most authoritative Sastra, and divine love is the

fifth and highest achievement of human life beyond

dharma, artha, kama and moksa. It is thus known as

paticama-purusartha. This is the verdict of Sti Cait-

anya Mahaprabhu and we have the highest regard

for this conclusion. (Caitanya-mata-mafijusa)

Thus, the student of Bhagavad-gita has little interest

in the first four goals of human society because all such

achievements ate temporary and do not lead to union

(yoga) with the Supreme Person. Only that which leads

one to Sti Krsna should be the goal of one’s life.
