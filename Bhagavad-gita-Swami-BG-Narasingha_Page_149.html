VERSE 36

अपि चेदसि पपिभ्यः सर्वेभ्यः पापकृत्तमः।

सर्व HABIT वृजिनं संतरिष्यसि ॥३६॥

api ced asi papebbyab sarvebhyab papa-krttamab

sarvatit fitana-plavenaiva vyfinam santarisyasi

Even if you are most impious, you can cross over the ocean

of all vices by boarding the boat of wisdom.

11 sarmiddho’gnir bhasmasat kurute’rjuna

frandgnib sarva-karmani bhasmasat kurute tatha

Asa blazing fire turns wood into ashes, similarly the fire of

wisdom renders all karma to cinders, O Arjuna.
