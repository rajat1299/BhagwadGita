VERSE 20

BSAA: प्रप्यन्तेऽन्यदेवताः ।

तं तं नियममास्थाय प्रकृत्या नियताः स्वया ॥२०॥

kamais tais tair वि prapadyante’nya-devatah

tarh tah niyamam asthaya prakrtya niyatah svaya

Those whose intelligence has been lost to various material

desires take shelter in other gods. Swayed by their natures

they perform various rituals.

VERSE 21

यो यो at या तनुं भक्तः श्रद्धया्चिंतुमिच्छति ।

तस्व तस्याचलां श्रद्धां तामेव विदधाम्यहम्‌ ॥२१॥

yo yo yar yarh tanurh bhaktab Sraddbayarchitum icchati

tasya tasyacalarn Sraddbarh tam eva vidadbamy-abam

Whatever form of the demigods a man desires to worship

with faith, I make his faith strong in that particular form.

VERSE 22

स तया श्रद्धया युक्तस्तस्याराधनमीहते ।

~ si

लभते च ततः कामान्मयेव विहितान्हि तान्‌ ॥२२॥

sa taya Sraddbaya yuktas tasyaradhanam thate

labbate ca tatah kaman mayaiva vibitan hi tan

One who worships that particular form with faith, only

attains the fulfilment of his desires due to My sanction alone.
