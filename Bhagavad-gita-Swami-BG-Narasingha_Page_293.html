VERSE 1

aR उवाच |

FEIT परमं गुह्यमध्यात्मसंज्ितम्‌ |

यत्वयोक्तं वचस्तेन मोहोऽयं विगतो मम ॥१॥

arjuna uvaca —

mad anugrabaya paramarm eubyam adbyatma-sarhjiitam

yat tvayoktath vacas tena moho’yarh vigato mama

Arjuna said: By Your mercy, You have explained the

greatest secret about Your supreme nature — thus my

bewilderment is gone.

VERSE 2

भवाप्ययौ हि भूतानां श्रुतौ विस्तरो मया ।

त्वत्तः कमलपत्राक्ष माहात्म्यमपि चाव्ययम्‌ ॥२॥

bhavapyayau bi bhutanarh Srutau vistaraso maya

tvatiah kamala-patraksa mahatmyam api cavyayam

O lotus-eyed Krsna, I have heard Your detailed description

of the creation and destruction of all living beings, as well

as Your eternal glories.

VERSE 3

एवमेतद्यथात्थ त्वमात्मानं परमेश्वर ।

द्रष्टुमिच्छामि ते रूपमेश्वरं पुरुषोत्तम ॥६॥

evam etad yathattha tvam atmanarh paramesvara

drastum icchami te ripam aisvaram purusottama

O Supreme Controller, what You have described about

Yourself is true. O Supreme Person, I now desire to see

Your form of great splendour.
