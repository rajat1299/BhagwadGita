9) mighty-armed warrior, learn from Me the five factors that

VERSE 14

अधिष्ठानं तथा कतां करणं च पृथग्विधम्‌ ।

विविघाश्च पृथकेषटा दैवं चैवात्र पञ्चमम्‌ ॥ ९४॥

adbisthanarh tatha karta karanarh ca prthag-vidham

vividbas ca prthak cesta daivarh caivatra paiicamam

The basis (the body), the performer of activities (the false

ego), the instrument (the senses), the different types of

endeavours and the Supreme Person — these are the five

factors that accomplish all actions.

VERSE 15

उारीरवा्यनोभिर्यत्कर्म प्रारभते नरः |

न्याय्य वा विपरीतं वा पञ्चैते तस्य हेतवः ॥ १५॥

Sartra-vaitmanobbir yat karma prarabbate narab

nyayyatit va viparttath va paficaite tasya hetavab

These five factors are the source of all actions, both good

and bad, that an embodied being experiences in this world.
