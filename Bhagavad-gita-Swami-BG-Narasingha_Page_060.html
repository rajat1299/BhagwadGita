ग BHAGAVAD-GITA oye

Bhagavan Sti Krsna said: While speaking like a wise man,

you are actually grieving for that which is unworthy of

grief. The wise lament for neither the living nor the dead.

VERSE 12

न त्वेवाहं जातु नासं न त्वं नेमे जनाधिपाः।

न चैव न भविष्यामः सर्वे वयमतः परम्‌ ॥ १२॥

1.111.111

na caiva na bhavisyamab sarve vayam atab param

There was never a time that you, nor I, nor all these war-

riors assembled here did not exist. Nor shall we ever cease

to exist in the future.

VERSE 13

देहिनोऽस्मिन्यथा देहे कोमारं योवनं जरा ।

तथा देहान्तरपरापिरधीरस्तत्र न मुह्यति ॥१६॥

debino’smin yatha debe kaumararh yauvanarh jara

tatha debantara praptir dbiras tatra na mubyati

As the atma passes through the bodily transformations of

childhood, youth and old. age, it similarly transmigrates

from one body to another at the time of death. The wise

are never deluded by this transition.
