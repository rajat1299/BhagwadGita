The great Bhagavad-gita commentator Sti Viévanatha

Caktavarti says that the power of Krsna to be eternally

unborn and at the same time take birth is due to Krsna’s

inconceivable energy (acintya-gakti). If Krsna were not

inconceivable (acintya) then He would not be the Supreme

Person. Vigvanatha confirms that one who understands

this is never bewildered and is free from all karma.
