VERSE 31

पवनः पवतामस्मि रामः शस्रभृतामहम्‌ ।

षाणां मकरश्चास्मि स्रोतसामस्मि जाहवी ॥३१॥

pavanab pavatam asmi ramab Sastra-bbrtam abam

fhasanan makaras casmi srotasam asmi jabnavi

Of purifiers I am the wind. Of the wielders of weapons I

am Ramacandra. Amongst aquatics I am the makara and of

rivers I am the Ganga.

VERSE 32

सर्गाणामादिरन्तश्च मध्यं चैवाहमर्जुन ।

अध्यात्मविद्या विद्यानां वादः प्रवदतामहम्‌ ॥६२॥

11/71 11

adbyatma-vidya 11011

O Arjuna, in creation I am the beginning, the middle and

the end. Of wisdom I am knowledge of the self and I am

the doctrine of philosophers.

VERSE 33

अक्षराणामकारोऽस्मि इन्दः सामासिकस्य च ।

अहमेवाक्षयः कारो धाताहं विश्वतोमुखः WRN

aksaranam akaro’smi dvandvab samasikasya ca

abam evaksayah kalo dhatabarh vifvato-mukbab

Of letters I am the letter A. Amongst compound words I

am the dual. I alone am the eternal flow of the time factor

and I am the Creator, who gazes in all directions.
