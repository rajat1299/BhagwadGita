The Seven Sages, the Four Kumaras and the Manus, from

whom all the living beings of this world have descended,

are all manifest from My mind.

VERSE 7

एतां विभूतिं योगं च मम यो वेत्ति तत्त्वतः ।

सोऽविकम्पेन योगेन युज्यते नात्र संशयः ॥७॥

शा vibbutith yogarh ca mama yo vetti tattvatab

so’vikampena yogena yujyate natra sarnSayab

One who knows the truth of My splendour and the process

of yoga becomes resolutely united with Me. Of this there

is no doubt.

Anuvrttt

All things, good and bad, happiness and distress,

fame and infamy ete. arise from Krsna, but this is not to

encourage a fatalistic approach to life. The bhakti-yogt is

encouraged to use his intelligence to discriminate between

favourable and unfavourable situations, accepting that

which is favourable for bhakti-yoga and rejecting that

which is unfavourable.

anukulyasya satkalpab pratikulyasya varjanam

One should accept those things that are favourable

for bhakti and reject those things that are anfavour-

able. (Hari-bhakti-vilasa 11.417)
