A seer of the truth is never degraded by the influences

of material natare. He gradually rises to perfection and

attains the Supreme Abode of Krsna. Those who are con-

ditioned by material nature and who have no knowledge

of Super Consciousness wrongly conceive that they are the

doer of activities, or that they are the overlords of material

natute. This however is a foolish idea since they themselves

are helplessly suffering at the hands of death.

For those that have the eyes to see, all forms of life

indicate the presence of a conscious living being. That

is to say that not just humans are conscious or that only

human consciousness is eternal. All things that manifest

birth, growth, maintenance, reproduction, dwindling and

death, regardless of higher birth (human) ot lower birth

(animal), are known as eternal beings transmigtating in

the material world. Thus, one who is friendly, kind and

compassionate must be so to all forms of life. It is not that

humans ate spared but animals and others can be killed

ot exploited for our pleasure. This idea falls short of the

vision of Bhagavad-gita which sees all living beings as part

and patcel of the Supreme Person, Krsna. Thus, all living

beings have a right to life.
