VERSE 34

मन्मना भव मद्धक्तो मद्याजी मां नमस्कुरु ।

मामेवेष्यसि युक्त्यैवमात्मानं मत्परायणः ॥ ३४॥

man-mana bhava mad bhakto mad yaji marh namaskuru

mam evaisyast yuktvaivam atmanath mat-parayanab

Always think of Me. Be My devotee. Worship Me. Offer

your respects unto Me. In this way, by dedicating yourself to

Me and surrendering to Me, you will come to Me.

Anuvttti

It is important to note here that Sti Kysna says that He

is equal to all living beings. That is to say that Krsna has

no ‘chosen people’. Everyone is equal in His eyes as His

eternal parts and parcels. Krsna does not arbitrarily cause

one person to enjoy life and another to suffer. Enjoyment

and suffering are the results of one’s own pious or impious

activities in this life and in past lives.

When one approaches Krsna, He reciprocates directly

with that person. One does not have to be born in a pat-

ticular country, family, religion, caste, gender or tace to

approach Krsna. Nor is one first required to perform

certain pious activities such as penances or charity before

one can apptoach Him. The door is open to everyone and

Krsna reciprocates with each individual accordingly.

But there is also no doubt that those who are deeply

devoted to Krsna and have taken shelter of Him com-

pletely, are very deat to Him and at the end of life they

will surely come to Krsna in His Supreme Abode.
