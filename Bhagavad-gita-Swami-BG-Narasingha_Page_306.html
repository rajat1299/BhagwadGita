ग BHAGAVAD-GITA oye

All the sons of Dhrtarastra, their royal allies, as well as

Bhisma, Drona, Karna and also the best fighters in our

army, are all hurtling into Your terrible mouths with their

frightening teeth. Some I see with their heads caught and

pulverised between Your teeth.

VERSE 28

यथा नदीनां बहवोऽम्बुवेगाः समुद्रमेवाभिमुखा द्रवन्ति ।

तथा तवामी नरलोकवीरा विशान्ति वक्राण्यभिविज्वलन्ति ॥२८॥

111

samudram evabbimukba dravanti

tatha tavami nara-loka-vira

visanti vaktrany-abbivijvalanti

Just as rivers flow towards the ocean and finally enter

it, similarly all these famous heroes enter into Your

fiery mouths.

VERSE 29

यथा Wale ज्वलनं Taw वियन्ति नाशाय समृद्धवेगाः ।

तथैव नाख्ाय विशन्ति रोकास्‌ तवापि वक्राणि समृद्धवेगाः ॥२९॥

yatha pradtptam jvalanath patanga

visanti nasaya samrddha-vegab

tathaiva naSaya visanti lokas

tavapi vaktrant samrddha-vegab

Just as moths rush to their deaths by entering fire, all the

worlds rush into Your mouths to their destruction.
