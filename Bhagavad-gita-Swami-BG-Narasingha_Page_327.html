Sy CHAPTER 12 स

VERSE I

aR उवाच |

परं सततयुक्ता ये भक्तास्त्वां पयुपासते ।

ये चाप्यक्षरमव्यक्तं तेषां के योगवित्तमाः ॥१॥

arjuna uvaca —

evatit sa tata~yukta ye bhaktas tvarn paryupasate

ye capy-aksaram avyak tah tesa ke yoga-vittamab

Arjuna said: Who is best situated in yoga — those who

constantly glorify You or those who are situated in Your

impersonal, imperishable aspect?

VERSE 2

श्रीभगवानुवाच |

मच्यावेक्य मनो ये मां नित्ययुक्ता उपासते |

श्रद्धया परयोपेतास्ते मे युक्ततमा मताः ॥२॥

Si bhagavan uvaca —

mayy-avesya mano ye mam nitya~yuk ta upasate

Sraddhaya parayopetas te me yuktatama matab

Bhagavan Sti Krsna replied: Those who fix their minds

upon Me, who constantly glorify Me and. possess great

faith — I consider them to be most perfect.

Anuvttti

Up to this point in Bhagavad-gita, Sti Krsna has

explained His personal, impersonal and universal aspects as

well as the various types of yogis. Now Arjuna asks for spe-

cial clarification as to which is the better path — the path of

bhakti-yoga centred on Kysna directly and performed with

acts of devotion, ot the impersonal path that tries to focus

on Krsna’s Brahman effulgence (brabma-jyoti).
