VERSE 1

श्रीभगवानुवाच |

इमं विवस्वते योगं परोक्तवानहमव्ययम्‌ |

विवस्वान्मनवे प्राह मनुरिश्चाकवेऽवीत्‌. ॥१॥

Sri bhagavan wvaca —

(4 vivasvate yogath prok tavan abam avyayam

vivasvan manave praha manur iksvakave’bravit

Bhagavan Sti Krsna said: I disclosed this imperishable

knowledge of yoga unto Vivasvan, the sun-god. He then

taught it to Vaivasvata Manu, who then instructed the

same knowledge to Tksvaku.

VERSE 2

एवं परम्पराप्ाप्तमिमं राजर्षयो विदुः ।

स कालेनेह महता योगो नष्टः परंतप ॥२॥

evath parampara praptam imarth rajarsayo vidub

sa kalencha mabata yogo nastab parantapa

In this way, O conqueror of the enemy, the pious rulers

understood this path of knowledge through the disciplic

succession. However, this knowledge of yoga has been lost

due to the influence of time.
