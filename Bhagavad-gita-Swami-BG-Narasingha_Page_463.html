Through such devotion that person knows Me in truth.

Thus, knowing Me in truth he enters My realm.

VERSE 56

सर्वकर्माण्यपि सदा कुर्वाणो मव्यपाश्रयः |

मत्परसादादवाध्रोति शाश्वतं पदमव्ययम्‌ ॥५६॥

sarva-karmany-api sada kurvano mad-vyapasrayab

mat-prasadad avapnoti SaSvatarh padam avyayam

Although one may constantly engage in various activities,

by My mercy, those who take shelter of Me teach My

eternal abode.

VERSE 57

चेतसा सर्वकर्माणि मयि संन्यस्य मत्परः |

बद्धियोगमुपाधित्य मच्चित्तः सततं भव ॥५७॥

cetasa sarva~karmani mayi sannyasya ma t-parab

buddbi-yogam upasritya mac-cittah satatarn bhava

Consciously renouncing all activities unto Me, considering

Me to be the supreme goal and taking shelter of the pro-

cess of devotion, always think of Me.
