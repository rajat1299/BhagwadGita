VERSE 68

य इदं परमं गृह्यं मद्धक्तेष्वमिधास्यति ।

भक्तिं मयि परां कृत्वा मामेवेष्यत्यसंशायः ॥६८॥

ya idath paramarh gubyarh mad -bhaktesv-abbidhasyati

bhaktirh mayi para krtva mam evaisyaty-asarhsayab

One who teaches this supreme secret of bhakti-yoga to

others, advances to the highest platform of devotion and.

attains full consciousness of Me. Of this there is no doubt.

na ca tasman manusyesu kaScin me priya-krttamab

There is no one dearer to Me in this world than such a

devotee. Nor will there ever be anyone dearer to Me than

one who teaches this supreme secret.
