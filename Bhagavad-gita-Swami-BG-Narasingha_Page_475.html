VERSE 73

aR उवाच |

नष्टो मोहः स्मृतिलव्धा त्वत्मसादान्मयाच्युत |

स्थितोऽस्मि गतसंदेहः करिष्ये वचनं तव ॥७३॥

arjuna uvaca —

nasto mobab smrtir labdba tvat prasadan mayacyuta

sthito’smi gata-sandehab karisye vacanarh tava

Arjuna replied: O infallible one, O Krsna, by Your mercy

my delusion has been dispelled and my mental equilibrium

has been restored. Now that my doubts have been removed.

I am steady once more and will follow Your advice.

Vasudeva and the great Arjuna that is so glorious that my

hairs stand. on end.
