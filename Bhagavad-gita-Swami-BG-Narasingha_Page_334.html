VERSE 9

अथ चित्तं समाधातुं न शक्रोषि मयि स्थिरम्‌ ।

अभ्यासयोगेन ततो मामिच्छाप्तुं घनञ्जय ॥९॥

atha cittarn samadbaturh na Saknosi mayi sthiram

abbyasa-yogena tato mam icchapturh dhanaftjaya

Dhanaijaya, if you cannot firmly fix your mind upon

Me, then try to reach Me by the constant practice of

bhakti-yoga.

VERSE 10

अभ्यासेऽप्यसमर्थोऽसि मत्कर्मपरमो भव |

मदर्थमपि कर्माणि कुर्वन्सिच्िमवाप्स्यसि ॥ ९०॥

abbyase’pyasamartho’si mat-karma-paramo bhava

mad-artham api karmani kurvan siddbim avapsyasi

If you cannot keep up the practices of bhakti-~yoga, then

just try offering your work to Me. Thus, you will reach the

perfect stage.
