recommended to control the mind by mantra. The word

mantra otiginates from two Sanskrit words — mana (the

the process of hearing and chanting mantras — and espe-

mind — the wandering mind becomes steady in the self.

The maba-mantra is a direct sound representation of

the Absolute Truth, Sti Krsna. As such, the maba-mantra

is both all-powerful and all-attractive. The power of the

maba-mantra removes the ignorance that covers the real

identity of the living being and the all-attractive nature

of the maba-mantra fills the heart of the chanter with

inconceivable ananda, supreme spititual bliss. Chanting of

the maba-mantra to control and steady the mind has been

highly recommended for these reasons.
