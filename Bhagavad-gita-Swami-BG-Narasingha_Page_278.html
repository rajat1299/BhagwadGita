VERSE 14

सर्वमेतदृतं मन्ये यन्मां वदसि केशव ।

नहि ते भगवन्व्यक्तिं विदुर्देवा न दानवाः ॥ ९४॥

sarvam. etad flath manye yan math vadasi keSava

na bi te bbagavan vyaktith vidur deva na danavab

O Keéava, everything You have told me I accept as truth.

O Master of all splendour, neither Demigods nor Danavas

can fully understand Your personality.

VERSE 15

स्क्यमेवात्मनात्मानं वेत्थ त्वं पुरुषोत्तम ।

भूतभावन भूतेश देवदेव जगत्पते ॥ १६॥

1/1. 1, vettha tvath purusottama

bbuta-bhavana bhutesa deva-deva jagat-pate

O Supreme Person, O Creator of all creatures, O Controller

of all living beings, O Master of the demigods, O Master of

the universe — You alone truly know Yourself.

VERSE I6

वक्तुमहस्यरेषेण दिव्या MIATA: |

याभिर्विमूतिभिर्कानि मांसत्वं व्याप्य तिष्ठसि ॥ १६॥

vaktum arbasy-asesena divya hy-atma-vibbutayab

yabbir vibbutibbir lokan imarhs tvarh vyapya tisthasi

Please describe to me completely Your divine powers by

which You pervade all the worlds.
